# You
()
Three letters I've wanted to
(forget and)
remember, so much that
(water rolls down)
my cheeks flush
(the color off them)
'til the pink stops
(to stain)
at the corners of my lips
(, tasting of salt).
I touch them,
(dying the tips of my fingers pink,)
remembering
(the isolation felt)
when your lips
(never)
parted,
(stinging me with silence,)
overwhelming me
(with alienation instead,)
from your sincerest actions.
