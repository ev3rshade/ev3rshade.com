> "Be sincere; be brief; be seated" - Franklin D. Roosevelt

As I was thinking about how I was going to vlog my journey to Philadelphia, I ended up thinking about how I am far more outspoken than I used to be

In the same moment, I realized that I have not outgrown being shy. Even as I find no trouble greeting new people and slipping into conversation with most, I still cannot bring myself to relax the control on my reactions and express how I truly feel about every topic.

I am not being honest with myself, and in that sense it is difficult for me to accept compliments from people I actually like because I am afraid I will get too attached.
