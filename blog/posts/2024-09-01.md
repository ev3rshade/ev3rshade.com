> "Blue is the only color which maintains its own character in all its tones. It will always stay blue." – Raoul Dufy 

a message sent to an acquaintance to explain my fascination with blue.

---

so for as far back as i can remember, blue has been my favorite color. 

whenever i'd get out into a big field or on top of a large hill, the sky would stretch for miles and miles, and i would stare at it for hours because i *knew* in those moments that the earth was spherical and that was the about the biggest revelation i'd had in my earliest years. in the amount of blue that existed in these moments, i felt infinitesimal. 

blue was a mystery, an endless possibility, and an impossible wish. 
breathtaking, wistful, and achingly beautiful.
