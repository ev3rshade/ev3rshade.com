i sit writing broken poetry

three years ago i wrote a poem. it wasn't about you.

it wasn't a good poem, but what strikes me every time about it is the title.

i titled it The Star and the Sea.
the Star was my old crush. the Sea was me.

but now you and the Star and i am still the Sea and no matter how hard i try i cannot escape the surface of earth. the best i can do is reflect the light from you that illuminates my waters i can never be next to you in the skies and i am stuck admiring you, waiting for your light to appear.
