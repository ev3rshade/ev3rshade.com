**The Stars Were Dead (revised)**

I peer into the sky
The feeling that I am looking for something throbs
It's been like this for awhile

I search

Pouring over
Books
Articles
Television
People
Desires
Wondering
Cosuming
*Everything*
Still wondering
What I am so desperate to find?

I have always been fascinated with media
It is produced with the intention to convey something
Although I am a single face in the billions, the word "audience" encompasses me as an individual as well.
I am looking for an answer through lines of communication 
between
creator and consumer
The consumer seeks answers
An experience
I am ravenously tearing away chunks of the world trying to find an answer
Trying to *feel* something other than
Boredom
Emptiness

I am drawn to the creations describe a realities in intensities so starkly contrasting from my own
As I drink the extravagance and agony within a fictious work
I observe what I cannot from the tangible world

When will I feel something again?

I turn to mimicking the world around me and miming the characters from my favorite shows
Fabricating a personality from the scraps of people I once knew
Because I don't believe in my own existence
Because WHO AM I
When my heart has forgotten to feel
And my thoughts are a figment of my own imagination

I'm tired now
I guess I should go to bed

But as I sit here burning my eyeholes into the fabric of the world
It only stared back
And I couldn't help but feel that something was wrong

The stars are dead

I wish the stars were alive like last night
I don't understand
I want to talk (to you)
(with you)
but i don't know how
I am yet again
Afraid
Because I am never sure whether the person across from me desires our interactions as much as I do
When did I begin to care for such things
Since when did I accept the invitation to the dance of modern socialization
Now I am trapped on the whirling dance floor
Trembling to the chatter of
Others

Kill the noise

My heart yearns for freedom
To chase its dreams
But it's hard to keep friends when you're flying solo all the time
And you are too hooked on people to be alone ever again
Because people value cooperation over the provocative individual

I inevitably remain
A loner
A seeker
And a curious soul
Qualities stacked in an arrangement vivid with confusion but awash with boredom
Because the search turns into the drone of repetition when you never find anything
And the curiously runs into despair when it is never satiated
Perhaps I will never find what I am looking for
And the small things will have to do

Will I be okay with that?
No
I don't think so
