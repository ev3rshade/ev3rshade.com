> "Autumn…the year's last, loveliest smile." - William Cullen Bryant

the cooler air always gets to me
wavering nostalgia that
crumbles to touch as i
am forced to consider

what dissuades me from opening up to people
when i love them so dearly
sad girl autumns
sting my skin


it was when i caught myself smiling (not because i felt happy but because it would distill the tension in the air within the group) that i wondered when i had learned to control my facial expressions

people view me as an open person, someone who's likes and dislikes were clearly painted on my face, but maybe that wasn't true

i hate people for being vapid

yet i cannot bring myself to provoke someone into peeling back the layers they shield themselves under

and i cannot begin to tell people that i do not understand how they feel

because i haven't felt anything for a very long time
