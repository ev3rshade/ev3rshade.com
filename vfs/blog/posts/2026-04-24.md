while the people many of the people that i have met over the course of the last two years are personable, conversational, and caring, i know that they or neither secure nor confident enough in themselves to approach a larger scope of people with kindness and an impartial, affable attitude. 

a larger scope including: 
the people that are not conventionally attractive. the people that are more aggressive and crude with their demeanor. the people who speak differently. the quiet, clingy, awkward people that spill their whole life to you. 

the relationships most people carve with people are characterized by their desire to leave a good impression after their conversation. their anxiety drives them to select the conversations they know will go well. these people drive away the remaining conversations with unspoken judgement and distance themselves, neglecting the opportunity to resolve misunderstandings, balance their values with a new perspective, and meet more people.

i remind you every human deserves to be listened to because they embody a story. 
open your ears and heart up and set your own feelings aside while they tell it to you.
